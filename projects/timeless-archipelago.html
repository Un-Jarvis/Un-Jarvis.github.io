Description

  • Project by Creaking C⚙️g Creations in Game Design and Development Course (CSE 5912) in 2019 at the Ohio State University.
  • Team Members: Wesley Brown, Jarvis Huang, Allie Jett, Sergey Maltsev, Pavan Nimmagadda, Haoran Wang.
    Director: Roger Crawfis.
  • A 3-D action role-playing game built with Unity3D. The game automatically generates multiple different islands, and has four island themes, four classes, five types of enemies, and four bosses.
  • In the game, the player is able to collect weapons and accessories with different stats by killing enemies and opening treasure chests. The player can manage items in inventory.
  • Each class has a unique skill tree where the player is able to enhance skills and abilities.
  • Some types of enemies are modeled with distinctive AI. Different bosses have different AI and mechanisms.
  • All game assets were purchased from Unity Asset Store.

Basic Enemy AI

  • Each enemy has a moving area which is a circle centered at a random point (the yellow circle showed in the gifs). The enemy won't move out of its moving area.
  • Each enemy has a field vision with some angle and distance (the red cone showed in the gifs). After detecting target in its vision, the enemy increases its moving speed, follows its target, and starts to attack its target.
  • The player can escape from enemies by running out of enemies' moving areas.
