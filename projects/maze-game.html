Maze Game

Description

  • A simple 3-D game built with Unity3D.
  • Players control the character to walk through a maze, avoid enemies, and collect coins.
  • The maze is automatically generated by recursive backtracking algorithm.
  • The enemy AI agents are modeled as predators with narrow field of view. Once the player object enters the enemyâ€™s field of view, the enemy will detect the player and find a path to chase after the player.
  • Before starting, the player can change settings of the game in the inspector of the Game Generator object, such as the size (number of rows and columns) of the maze, the number of enemies and coins.
